Overview

The program, the project, and everything in between.

The Program

Six weeks, Cape Town

I traveled to Cape Town, South Africa for a six-week study abroad internship, where I served as a Software Engineering Intern alongside teammates Andre and Shreya.

The Project

MERLS, for GRIT

We built MERLS (Monitoring, Evaluating, Researching and Learning System) — a full-stack grant-management dashboard for GRIT (Gender Rights in Tech), a Cape Town-based nonprofit, replacing their spreadsheet-based workflow.

Beyond the Code

A bigger picture

From Table Mountain to the Khayelitsha township, the program immersed me in South Africa's history and culture — and reshaped how I think about building technology that serves real communities.
